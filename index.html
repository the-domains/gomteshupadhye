Pallivalu bhadravattakam - A Musical Tribute to Women

Gomtesh Upadhye / YouTube
Pallivalu bhadravattakam is a Music Video celebrating womanhood. A Malayalam Folklore Depicting Godess Kali fighting over evil . Music direction and arrangements by Sanjeev Thomas with lead vocals by Sayanora Philip. The video has been shot in the beautiful state of Karnataka and the climax in Kerala.
